# I hate myself because of ADHD

- I hate how I interrupt conversations. 

- I hate how I blurt stuff out. 

- I hate all the stupid things I say. 

- I hate all the awkward things I do in front of people.

- I hate that feeling when I'm smart enough to know how awkward I am, but not smart enough to know how not to be awkward.

- I always make a fool of myself when I say anything to anybody. 

- The moment I get excited about what I’m saying, whether that be actual excitement or frustration or anything, I don’t even realise I just keep talking and talking and often quite loudly.

- I hate how I can't even shut up even after catching myself doing these stupid things.

- I hate how I can't articulate my thoughts properly.

- I hate how I have short-term memory loss in the middle of saying something.

- I hate how I start talking while still unsure of what I'm going to say and then cut off abruptly. I hate how the gibberish words just fade out ridiculously. 

- I hate how miserably hard I try to make people understand my situation and fail. 

- I hate how I dump info on people (especially Anha now) for no reason even if nobody asked me something remotely related to it.

- I hate how I try to overexplain everything and how savagely I get ignored in the middle of it.

- I hate how I can't focus on even a single thought, let alone task.

- I hate how I end up doing nothing when I've got so much to do, even things I'd love to do. 

- I hate how terribly I procrastinate on my tasks and even things I love (reading) or even things as normal as peeing or shitting. 

- I hate how I'm growing older, but not more mature.

- Since I'm an introvert, I should stay that way and not try to be like extroverts. Maybe being mute would solve all my problems. (Not really, just an exaggeration)
